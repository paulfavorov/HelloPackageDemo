Infrastructure built for porches, not for floor 14, unit 1407. The number of destinations grew exponentially while every building's mailroom stayed the same size. TREND TWO — 21.2 billion packages a year in the U.S., and 35% of them route to multifamily. The volume isn't the only problem — residents now expect their packages on demand. Your leasing and concierge teams are revenue-generating roles. They drive renewals, rent growth, resident satisfaction. NMHC pegs the time they lose to packages at 10-20 hours per week. That's a third of their week, gone to sorting boxes. Overworked. Stressed. Burned out. And every hour comes out of NOI. Three trends. One broken mailroom. That's what we built HelloPackage to fix.

The problem 03 / 13
Four symptoms your team hears about every week. Lockers overflow — they were sized for 2015 volume. They can't handle 2026. The hardware doesn't grow with the building. Closets are unsecured — when lockers fill, staff stuff parcels in a leasing back-room. No camera, no audit, no chain of custody. When packages walk off, you have no recourse. Manual labor is not scalable — throwing concierge hours at the problem works for 50 packages a day, not 500. The labor cost goes linear while the volume goes exponential. The math gets worse every quarter. Frustration causing turnover — and not just resident turnover. Leasing teams burn out. Concierges quit. Online reviews tank. The package problem becomes a hiring problem becomes a NOI problem. Under all of this: e-commerce keeps growing 11% annually. Whatever you have today won't be enough next year.

How It Works
How it works

Four steps, every package.

Step 01
Driver guidance.

Automatically grant access and train drivers to follow a guided process and log every package as they arrive. 98.7% driver participation — no more "dump and go".

Step 02
Logged & stored.

Multiple indoor kiosks and computer vision track every drop-off in real time. Stored in the secure, access-controlled room with cameras on.

Step 03
Resident alerts.

Pickup instructions sent automatically. 24/7 secure access with a code.

Step 04
Automated security monitoring.

Every interaction recorded. Full chain-of-custody. Package volume, resident duration, and room state visible to property in the Dashboard.
